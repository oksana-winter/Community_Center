ABOUT US

Mission & Vision

It is the people who constitute the lifeblood of our flourishing community, and the Bern Community Center stands as a distinguished nexus for all denizens of Bern. It is a venerable gathering place—where minds convene, knowledge is both imparted and acquired, and the rich tapestry of shared experience is woven ever tighter.

Here, one remains not merely informed but profoundly attuned to the pulse of the community: its aspirations, its dialogues, and the myriad ways in which each individual may extend support—or graciously receive it.

History

Founded in 1985, the BERN COMMUNITY CENTER has been serving our community for over three decades. What started as a small gathering place has grown into a vibrant hub of activity and connection.

Throughout the years, we've expanded our facilities and programs to meet the evolving needs of our community, always staying true to our core values of inclusivity and service.
